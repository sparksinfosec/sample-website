Summer Camp Magic Puffs

Ingredients:
  1. First you will want preheat the oven to 375 F.
  2. Now remove the cresent rolls from the can and mashmellows from packaging - set aside.
  3. In one small bowl mix together the white sugar and cinnamon until nicely blended together.
  4. Take a single marshmellow and dip it into the melted butter, and then roll it in the cinnamon sugar.
  5. Now set the covered marshmellow onto a single piece of cresent roll. Roll the dough around the marshmellow until completely covered (Important: make sure the dough is sealed around the marshmellow)
  6. Now roll the dough covered marshmellows in the melted butter, and then in the cinnamon sugar
  7. Place the dough covered marshmellow to a bakeing pan or cup cake pan. Leave about an inch inbetween for expansion.
  8. Repeast step 1-7 until you are out of cresent rolls, marshmellows, or pan space.
  9. Once all marshmellows are wrapped and coated in cinnamon sugar and placed on the baking trey. Insert into the oven for 10-15 minutes
  10. After the puff have baked and are golden brown, carefully remove from oven and let cool
  11. Optional: Add frosting, powdered sugar or more cinnamon sugar while cooling.
  12. ENJOY!
